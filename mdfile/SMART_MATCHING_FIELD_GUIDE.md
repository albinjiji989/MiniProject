## ğŸ” Testing Methods

### Energy Level
1. Play with pet for 30 minutes
2. Observe: Still bouncing? = High energy. Tired? = Low/Moderate
3. Track daily activity for 1 week

### Child-Friendly
1. Invite children (ages 3, 6, 10, 15) separately
2. Supervise interaction (tail wagging? Hiding? Gentle mouth?)
3. Video record for review
4. Test 3+ times before scoring

### Pet-Friendly
1. Arrange playgroups with different dogs/cats
2. Observe: Aggression? Fear? Play bows? Sharing toys?
3. Test with at least 5 different animals
4. Watch for resource guarding (food, toys)

### Separation Anxiety
1. Set up camera
2. Leave pet alone for 30 min, 1 hr, 2 hrs, 4 hrs
3. Review footage: Crying? Destruction? Calm?
4. Repeat 3+ times for reliability
